What are the beneifits of  using meta tag?

SEO Improvement: Meta tags, such as meta descriptions and meta keywords, help search engines understand the content and purpose of web pages, potentially improving search engine rankings.

Enhanced Click-Through Rates: A well-crafted meta description can entice users to click on your website in search results by providing a concise summary of the page's content.

Control Over Display: Meta tags allow you to control how your website appears in search engine results pages (SERPs), social media platforms, and other online channels, influencing users' perceptions and behavior.

Better User Experience: Clear meta descriptions and titles provide users with accurate expectations of what they will find on a web page, leading to higher user satisfaction and engagement.

Accessibility: Some meta tags, like meta viewport, improve the accessibility and usability of websites across various devices, ensuring a consistent and optimized experience for all users.
